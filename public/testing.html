Tips
  • Get points for killing other players and use them for upgrades.
  • Follow the ingame instructions to know what you have to do.
  • Use the minimap for directions so you don't get lost.
  • Collect the boost tanks to refill your boost.

Controls
Press the space button to accelerate
Shift slows you down with some backwards thrust
Move your mouse and the spaceship will rotate
Click the left mouse button to shoot lasers
Use the right mouse button to activate your boost
